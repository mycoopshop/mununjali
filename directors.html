Our Board of Directors

Ashley Currie

Chairperson

Ashley has been a committee member since the '80s and has witnessed the growth of Mununjali from being one of the minor organisations to one the most respected. Ashley has been instrumental in overseeing many changes in the management and the development of HACC services and support programs. Over the years, Ashley's skills have improved. Now, as District Coordinator Service Planning & Development, A&TSI Health Professional Manager, South East Cardiac Clinical Network, Ashley can see how this growth compliments his role.

Family history: Aboriginal Father and Mother of English Heritage

Interests include: Community and family

Favourite things to do: Camping and spending time with family and friends

Employment History: Tancred Brothers Beaudesert, Katherine meat works, Mt Isa, Kilcoy, Chislom Manufacturing, Bc Boning, Wild Game, Private Builder, Gold Coast Hospital, and Beaudesert

Brad Currie

General Manager

Community Organisations need stability. Sometimes the decisions are made for the best interests of all community. Brad has seen many organisations fail and get wound up due to inappropriate Corporate Governance. Having enjoyed a four-year tenure on the RTA Board with its professional development and exposure to exceptional Governance practices have enabled Brad to share the experience. Holding Positions on CDEP Board and various others has provided a solid footing for Business and Community Organisations Business practices.

Family history: Father Joe Currie, Mother Teresa Currie, Born and Bred In Beaudesert

Interests include: Family and Music

Favourite things to do: Music

Employment History: First Employee of Mununjali 1987, AMH , Group Training Australia

Other Roles: Ex-RTA Qld Director, SEQ Indigenous Housing Chairman, Golddoc IWG, JP Qual.
