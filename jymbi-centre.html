Jymbi Centre

The Mununjali Jymbi Centre serves the Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander people of the Beaudesert Shire. We help families and our cultural community development through healing and interventions and family and youth support.

Mununjali Jymbi Centre

Promoting community, cultural living and connecting with place and land.

Jymbi provides information and ongoing healing and support for Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander youth and families living in the Beaudesert area of Queensland.

Services offered include:

  • having a cuppa and a yarn
  • court support
  • counselling
  • referrals
  • day and overnight programs
  • women's and men's groups
  • client support services.
Contact Jymbi Centre

The Jymbi Staff

Our staff have a diversity of skills and life experience and are from Beaudesert and beyond.

In all our work, we aim to provide training and support to local community people building on the community's strengths.

All Jymbi Centre staff have the necessary qualifications for their roles.
